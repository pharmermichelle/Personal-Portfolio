How to Play

The world throws chaos at you fast. Read the colors, grab the right items, and keep moving.

Controls

  • Drag anywhere on the game area to move.
  • Stay away from red danger items.
  • Grab healing, shields, support, and upgrades.

Color guide

  • 🟢 Green: healing and regen
  • 🔴 Red: damage and debuffs
  • 🟡 Gold: shields and support
  • 🔵 Blue: focus and gear

Danger effects

  • Drain: score drops over time
  • Slow: movement gets sluggish
  • Confuse: controls invert briefly
  • Damage: lose hearts

Helpful effects

  • Regen: recover hearts over time
  • Calm: slows chaos down
  • Shield: blocks the next hit
  • Support: heals and clears bad effects
