Overview

As part of the Common Ground World (CGW) live-ops team, I led the design, management, and community communication for daily in-game events, centered around blockchain mechanics and NFT-based token rewards.

CGW is a blockchain-based town simulation game where players complete challenges to earn tokens. Each day, players who completed the posted challenge using qualifying NFTs received a share of a dynamic daily token pool, influenced by the total number of completions and NFTs used across the player base.

My team and I collaboratively developed the tokenomics and challenge structures (reference: Litepaper available).

Daily and Weekly Content Flow

  • Created and launched 7 new daily challenges every week
  • Designed 7 unique map layouts aligned to the week's "Meta"
  • Built 7 daily NFT challenges balanced around the current token pool economy
  • Published weekly event overview announcements via Discord, and daily posts across X, Instagram, and Facebook
  • Collaborated with QA to ensure maps and challenges functioned as intended before live deployment
