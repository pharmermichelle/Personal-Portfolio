Town Star (Common Ground World) ‚Äì Fall 2024

In Fall 2024, I designed and coordinated the Banana Oasis live-ops event for Town Star, blending player feedback, seasonal storytelling, strategic map design, and NFT integration to create an engaging, monetized gameplay experience.

Tagline: Get ready to go bananas in the dry heat! Can you rise to the challenge and bake the most Banana Bread without breaking a sweat?

Origin of Idea: Inspired by a community survey where "bananas" ranked as a top requested content idea. I led the initiative to bring it to life, using a data-driven, player-first approach.

  • Community engagement and retention
  • Live-ops event planning and execution
  • Cross-functional collaboration
  • Content creation and event storytelling
  • Data-informed content development
  • Event-driven game economy design
  • Strategic map design for live events
  • Social media marketing and Discord management
  • NFT monetization strategy
  • Player acquisition and lifecycle retention strategies

Content & Communications

Phase 1: New Items and Crafts

  • Banana Tree & Banana resource added to farming system
  • New Craft: Banana Split (ingredients include multiple ice cream cones and chocolate bar)
  • Coordinated social and Discord announcements

Phase 2: Craft Expansion

  • New Craft: Banana Bread (banana, flour, egg, sugar, butter)
  • Legacy NFTs (Bakery, Ice Cream Parlor) repurposed to boost value

Phase 3: Monetization

  • Released the Banana Monkey Tree NFT (passively generates bananas)
  • Balanced perks to maintain fairness and encourage optional purchases

Map & Gameplay Design

Map: Desert Biome with Saltwater Edge

  • Low natural water created banana farming challenges
  • Saltwater allowed for key craft components
  • Minimal tree requirements aligned with craft demands
  • Scarcity balanced to nudge NFT usage without mandating it

Community Strategy

  • Content inspired directly by survey feedback
  • Staggered releases sustained multi-week engagement
  • Thematic social and Discord engagement
  • Live Town Halls: sneak peeks, AMAs, giveaways
  • Monitored feedback in real time and adjusted accordingly

Cross-Functional Collaboration

  • Worked with Art Team on new assets
  • Collaborated with Design and Economy for balance
  • Aligned promotions with Marketing

Results

  • DAU increase during event
  • High engagement on Discord and Twitter
  • Community praised legacy NFT use
  • Exceeded NFT sales KPIs
  • Boosted session length and return play

Key Takeaway

The Banana Oasis event showcases my ability to merge community-driven design, live-ops execution, progressive storytelling, fair monetization, and cross-team collaboration into a highly engaging live event. It reflects my passion for building strong player communities and delivering sustainable gaming experiences.
