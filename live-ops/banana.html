Overview

In Fall 2024, I designed and coordinated the Banana Oasis live-ops event for Common Ground World, blending player feedback, seasonal storytelling, strategic map design, and NFT integration to create an engaging, monetized gameplay experience.

Tagline for the event:
"Get ready to go bananas in the dry heat! Can you rise to the challenge and bake the most Banana Bread without breaking a sweat?"

Origin of Idea: The event was inspired by a community survey where "bananas" ranked as a top requested content idea. I led the initiative to bring this idea to life, demonstrating a data-driven, player-first approach to live event design.

Content Additions

Phase 1: New Items and Crafts

  • Banana Tree & Banana resource introduced into the farming system
  • New Craft: Banana Split (2 Banana, 1 Chocolate Ice Cream Cone, 1 Vanilla Ice Cream Cone, 1 Strawberry Ice Cream Cone, 1 Chocolate Bar)
  • Coordinated social media and Discord announcements

Phase 2: Craft Expansion

  • Leveraged older NFTs (Bakery, Ice Cream Parlor) for crafting to enhance legacy asset value

Phase 3: Monetization

  • Released the Banana Monkey Tree NFT (passively generates bananas)
  • Balanced NFT benefits to maintain game fairness and encourage voluntary purchases

Map and Gameplay Design

Map Chosen: Desert Biome with Saltwater Edge

  • Low natural water to make banana farming challenging
  • Saltwater available to support other crafting needs
  • Minimal tree requirements aligned with craft demands
  • Strategic scarcity incentivized NFT acquisition without making it mandatory

Community Strategy

  • Phased releases to maintain multi-week player engagement
  • Thematic social media campaigns and Discord engagement activities
  • Live Town Halls with sneak peeks, AMAs, and giveaways
  • Real-time feedback monitoring and responsive event adjustments

Cross-Functional Collaboration

  • Partnered with Design and Economy teams to ensure balance

Results

  • High engagement on Discord and Twitter (X)
  • Positive community sentiment around legacy NFT use and event design
  • Exceeded internal KPIs for NFT sales
  • Extended player session length and repeat engagement over multiple weeks
